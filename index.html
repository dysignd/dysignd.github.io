Fig. 03 — Process / Method

Your story already exists.

We just provide the scaffolding to support what you've already built.

01

Survey

We start by reading the terrain — your organisation, your audience, your market. Conversations, existing materials, and research: we map what's really going on before we touch the brand.

02

Draft

Then we design the structure: positioning, narrative, and messaging frameworks your team can actually work from. This is the blueprint — the system that keeps every brochure, website, and video saying the same thing.

03

Build In

Most agencies hand you a deck and call it done. We're not satisfied until the story is yours — not ours. We embed the thinking into your systems, coach the team, and only step back when you're telling your own story better than we could.

Fig. 05 — Studio / About

We are the scaffolding.

Most of the briefs we encountered early on called for the same things. Photography. Video. A new website. A rebrand. And we delivered them.
But they changed very little.

What the teams around us actually needed wasn't another asset. It was clarity — about what they were doing, and a strategy for how to communicate it. Not just to attract clients, but to build teams that believed in what they were building.

byShanks&Son exists to serve that need.

The "by" in our name is intentionally lowercase. We are not the focus.

Big Ben was covered in scaffolding for years. That was never the goal. The goal was to let it keep standing — for centuries to come.

That's us. We are the scaffolding. Not the building.

Our job is to find what's already there, build the structure around it, and hand it back to you — so your team can carry it forward without us.

The ampersand in our logomark says the same thing. We come alongside. We enable. We help you build something that lasts.

Then we step back.
