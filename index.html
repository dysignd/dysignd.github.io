Brand Advisory

How we work

Every engagement starts
with a conversation.

Not a proposal. Not a pitch. A clear-headed look at where your brand is and what it actually needs.

01

Listen & learn

We start by understanding your organisation, your audience, and the gap between where you are and where you need to go — before we recommend anything.

02

Find the story

Your brand story isn't invented. It's already there — in your founders' intentions, your team's culture, your clients' outcomes. We surface it, and then we name it.

03

Build the system

Strategy without a system stays on paper. We turn the thinking into a usable framework: positioning, messaging, content pillars, communication guidelines.

04

Support the team

We don't hand over a document and disappear. We help your people understand it, use it, and adapt it — so it actually changes how you communicate.

About

Small studio.
Serious thinking.

by Shanks & Son is a brand and content strategy studio based in Hilton, KwaZulu-Natal. We work with South African organisations that know their brand matters — and are ready to do something about it.

We exist for one reason: to give local brands access to the kind of strategic thinking that has been reserved for corporates with large agency retainers. Enterprise-level thinking. Independent studio rates.

The "by" is deliberate — lowercase, editorial. We're not the story. You are. Our job is to listen carefully, find what's already true about your brand, and build the system that lets you tell it consistently.

We work with a small number of clients at a time. That's by design.
